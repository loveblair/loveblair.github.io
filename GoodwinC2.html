Red

Red can bring a feeling of excitement and when placed strategically, can be a call to action that causes a user to buy.

Orange

I think orange can evoke respect and trust. Especially, when it is paired with blue. It's worth noting that some studies show this as a least favorite color among both men and women.

Yellow

Yellow is a cheerful color but too much of it can be jarring to the eyes. It's a happy color, but creates a strong, not-so-fun contrast.

Green

Green is a favorable color. You notice a lot of organic brands being in green. It reminds me of greenery and money.

Blue

Blue evokes a sense of trust and loyalty It feels serene when you look at it.

Purple

Purple feels comforting and regal. I like purple. It is almost my favorite color. Most women find the color purple attractive.

Black

Is dark and serious. It is very conventional and classic. What is even better is it shows up nicely against white background.

Pink

Pink is a color that is soft, assured and friendly. It is my favorite color. I read somewhere that pink is the least favorite color among males.
